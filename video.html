Morse Code and Coding Information

The telegraph transfers the character and numbers by a switch. Two types of closing were used by an operator, short closing and long closing. Closing the switch for a short time was creating a short beep known as a dot. Closing the switch for a longer time was creating longer beep known as dash. All character coded by dot and dash, known as morse code.

ASCII Code

Capital letters small letters and symbols are coded too, these codes are called ASCII codes and they are known in computers. For example when you are sending a text on your cell phone, the text you enter, will be translated to these codes by your device, then they will be received as bunch of 01 and one by your friend and your friend device converts these codes to the character that you read.
